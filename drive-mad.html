About Drive Mad

Drive Mad is a challenging driving game with obstacle stages, skillful balancing on the track, and beautiful visual impressions. Experience unique roads, vivid graphics and sounds, and complete the goal of conquering the finish line of every level.

Drive Mad Gameplay:

Explore challenging terrain, steep slopes, and complex obstacles that increase the levels' difficulty.
Players experience many types of racing cars in the Drive Mad game and carry out the goal of moving safely toward the finish line. Show your accuracy and skills when accelerating, braking, and drifting to avoid collisions.
Each track will bring a different challenge, and the new type of racing car also challenges your skillful control skills. Take advantage of the terrain and full features of the car to overcome the challenge easily.

How to control:

  • Use the W/D or up arrow keys or click to accelerate.
  • Press A/S or the down arrow to reverse and balance.

Highlight Features:

Vivid Graphics and Sound:

The game has beautiful graphics that bring a professional racing environment and unique, meticulous design. The realistic sound of racing car engines and collisions further enhances the excitement for players.

Interesting obstacle levels:

The game continuously updates 100 seasonal and event levels to bring players new terrain experiences. The rich track landscape and obstacles also stimulate players to conquer the finish line.
Practice driving on the track from First Gear to Rope Bridge, Slippery Slope, and So Much Win.

Simple control keys:

Players can flexibly use the arrow keys and mouse to press the accelerator or brake when necessary. The racing car in the game will quickly take the control keys from you.

Tips to conquer the finish line in the game Drive Mad:

To conquer 100 challenging driving levels in the game, players must get used to the first level and overcome it easily. You should not rush; move slowly to learn about the challenges on the road. Avoid falling into the water or flipping over; then you will have to start over.
Practice driving skills by combining the two control keys to keep the car balanced on every track and reach the finish line.

The game Drive Mad brings a great entertainment space with interesting graphics and sounds. Combined with a unique playing style, Drive Mad is suitable for all players who love driving and enjoy the endless joy of victory.
